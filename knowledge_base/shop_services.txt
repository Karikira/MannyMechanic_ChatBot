ABOUT THE SHOP
--------------
Santos Auto Repair has been serving Davao City and surrounding areas since 2008. We specialise in Japanese and Korean vehicles, which make up the majority of cars on Davao roads. Our team of PRC-licensed automotive technicians handles everything from routine maintenance to major engine overhauls. We serve customers from Matina, Toril, Talomo, Buhangin, Mintal, Calinan, and as far as Panabo, Tagum, and Digos.

We are an authorised service partner for ACDelco parts and use only genuine or OEM-equivalent parts on all repairs. All work comes with a 30-day labour warranty and parts warranty as specified by the manufacturer.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS
---------------------------

Q: How long does a standard oil change take?
A: Usually 30 to 45 minutes. Faster with an appointment.

Q: Do I need an appointment?
A: Walk-ins are welcome but appointments are preferred for major services. Call 083-123-4567 or message us on Facebook.

Q: Do you offer pick-up and delivery of vehicles?
A: Yes, within Davao City for PHP 300-500 depending on distance. Free pick-up for bills over PHP 5,000.

Q: My car is still under dealer warranty. Can I service it here?
A: Yes. Republic Act 10642 (Philippine Lemon Law) allows you to have your car maintained at any accredited shop without voiding the warranty, as long as we follow the manufacturer-specified service intervals and use approved parts. We provide documentation for your records.

Q: What is your warranty on repairs?
A: 30 days on all labour. Parts warranty follows the manufacturer's terms (typically 3-12 months). If anything we repaired fails within the warranty period, we fix it at no additional labour charge.

Q: Do you handle insurance claims?
A: Yes. We are accredited with Prudential Guarantee, Standard Insurance, and Mapfre Philippines. We provide repair estimates for insurance purposes.

Q: What areas do you serve?
A: Located in Matina, Davao City. We serve customers from Toril, Talomo, Buhangin, Panacan, Lasang, Calinan, Mintal, Panabo, Tagum, and Digos.

Q: Do you work on hybrid vehicles?
A: We handle basic maintenance on hybrids like Toyota Vios hybrid, Toyota Yaris Cross hybrid, and Honda Jazz hybrid. We do not currently service high-voltage EV battery systems.
